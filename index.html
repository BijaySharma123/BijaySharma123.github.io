In the echoes of my verses, I am but a humble observer of nature's whispers and the unseen realms of emotion. Like the great bard Shakespeare, I am drawn to the essence of existence itself. Through my poetry, whether it be the murmurs of the woods or the flight of a beautiful bird, I seek to unveil the hidden truths of life’s journey. Each line penned is a reflection of my longing for serenity amidst turmoil and my quest for the elusive landscapes where love and peace flow like rivers unseen. In the silence and chaos of my verses, I strive to capture the essence of joy unseen and find solace in the pristine purity of expression. Through my art, I sow seeds of contemplation in barren fields, aiming to illuminate the paths where chaos yields to peaceful silence. I am Bijay Sharma, and in my smile lies the quintessence of heaven, mirrored in the lyrical tapestry of my words.
